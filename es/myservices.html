Is your web presence working for you?

If someone needs what you provide, will your website draw them in? What will they see when they're on their phone? A responsive and accessible web presence is no longer a 'nice to have' - it is required. .

I would like to offer you the chance to be seen on the internet, whether you have a business, non-profit or new venture. We can work together to build a website from the ground up or update an existing presence for this mobile era. How can I help you today?

My Services in more detail

I will listen to your vision, needs, and wants for your website, and I make that a reality. If you already have a website I can guarantee that it will have a more modern look and feel. The website that I build for you will be accessible such that anyone, whether they're on Mac, PC, or on a mobile device, will have a pleasant experience on your website. The expected process of what we can expect is below.

  1. Free Consultation - Send me an email, or contact me with your inquiry. This is a conversation where we talk about your business and pinpoint what this project is about.
  2. Proposal - A detailed project proposal including a visual preview will be presented. We will also work on timelines for the delivery of the product during this stage.
  3. Development - I will work on your website diligently and provide weekly updates with the progress that is being made. During this time I will be continually incorporating your feedback into your website.
  4. QA & Launch - After development is complete I will ensure that the website passes all the necessary checks for it to be ready for the public eye and we will launch the website live.
  5. Continued Support - Depending on what we agree upon during the proposal I offer continued support to ensure that the website continues to meet your changing needs.
