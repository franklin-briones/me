Biography

I grew up in the VERY sunny state of Florida ☀️. I went to Rice University in Houston, TX where I studied Biomedical Engineering at our world-renowned program. Throughout my time I learned a lot about the engineering process and I credit my 4 years in Houston as one of the most formative times in my life. I live in Madison, Wisconsin with my girlfriend, Izabella, and 2 cats, Mila and Evie. As of January of 2022 I've been honing my skills as a software developer focused on full-stack development.




So how do I go from Biomedical Engineering to Software Development? Due to the nature of my curriculum I often did not interact with software, so I independently took every opportunity I could to expose myself to code. I won 2 awards at a Datathon for creating a data visualization tool to determine which parts of Houston were most likely to adopt Telehealth appointments. Everytime I interacted with code, whether that was Python, MATLAB, or HTML I felt a rush that other work doesn't give me. I finished my 4 years at Rice to become a Biomedical Engineer, and with this passion in me I have been dedicating all of my waking hours to becoming the best software developer I can be.
